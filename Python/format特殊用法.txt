位置键：
{{{code: lang="python3" linenumbers="False"
"{0}_{1}".format(a, b)
}}}

名称键：{{{code: lang="python3" linenumbers="False"
"{a}_{b}".format(a=a, b=b)
}}}


传入对象用名称键：
"{date.year}{date.month}".format(date=datetime.datetime(year=2019, month=2))
 '{var}'.format(var=123)

约束宽度：
"{a:2d}".format(a=1) # -> " 1"
"{a:0>2d}".format(a=1) # -> "01"

"{:02}".format(1)  # -> "01"
