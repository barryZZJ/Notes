====== barrier ======
Created 星期五 25 十月 2024

用于进程同步，确保所有进程都达到了某个指定节点
https://docs.python.org/3.9/library/threading.html#barrier-objects

barrier = Barrier(parties: int, action=None)
action: 某一个进程在释放前会执行该操作
.wait() -> int  返回第i个到达该barrier的id，0~parties-1
