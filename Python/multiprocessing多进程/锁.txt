====== 锁 ======
Created 星期三 10 七月 2024

https://docs.python.org/3.9/library/multiprocessing.html#synchronization-between-processes
l = Lock()
1. with块
with l:
	synchronized job...

2. 手动加解锁
l.acquire()
l.release()

与pool结合使用：
{{{code: lang="python3" linenumbers="False"
def init_worker(lock):
    global shared_lock
    shared_lock = lock

lock = multiprocessing.Lock()
with multiprocessing.Pool(processes=num_processes, initializer=init_worker, initargs=(lock,)) as pool:
    rets = pool.map(worker_function, args) # 在worker_function里调用shared_lock
}}}
