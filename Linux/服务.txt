====== 服务 ======
创建日期 星期一 25 三月 2024

注册为服务：
apt install systemd
sudo nano /etc/systemd/system/frpc.service
[Unit]
Description=Frp Client
After=network-online.target
Wants=network-online.target

[Service]
Type=simple
Restart=always    
RestartSec=10
#ExecStartPre=/bin/sleep 30    # Add a delay of 30 seconds before starting
ExecStart=/usr/bin/frpc -c /home/barryoffice/frp/frpc_yy.ini
WorkingDirectory=/home/barryoffice/frp

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target

Restart=on-failure/always(结束后自动重启)

修改后需要 sudo systemctl daemon-reload
systemctl start frpc
自启动：systemctl enable frpc

单次运行脚本：
[Unit]
Description=SSHFS Mount Service
After=network-online.target
Wants=network-online.target

[Service]
Type=oneshot
ExecStart=/path/to/sshfs_mount.sh

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
