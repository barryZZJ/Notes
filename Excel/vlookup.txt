====== vlookup ======
Created 星期日 30 七月 2023

VLOOKUP(lookup_value, table_array, col_index_num, [range_lookup])

A	B
Apple	Fruit
Banana	Fruit
Carrot	Vegetable
Tomato	Vegetable
Potato	Vegetable

=VLOOKUP("Carrot", A:B, 2, FALSE)  # ==the cell will display "Vegetable," 
在A:B中搜索"Carrot"，返回第2列，FALSE为精确查找

变为数字：D1*1
变为字符：D1&""
