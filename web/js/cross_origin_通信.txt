1. 使用window.postMessage方法（收发窗口必须在同一个页面）
https://www.runoob.com/js/met-win-postmessage.html
https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Window/postMessage
https://blog.csdn.net/chituZip/article/details/82350798

发送页面：
receiverWindow.postMessage(msg, sender's host)
window.parent.postMessage(jsonstr, url)
接受页面：
window.addEventListener("message", function(event){
    var data = event.data; // 确认event.origin == 来源网址
}, false);
