====== zim深色样式调整 ======
创建日期 星期三 02 七月 2025

Linux:
cp /usr/share/zim/style.conf ~/.config/zim/
nano ~/.config/zim/style.conf

Windows: $XDG_CONFIG_HOME见environ.ini
$XDG_CONFIG_HOME/config/zim/style.conf

[Tag code]
foreground=#2e3436
foreground[darktheme] = lightgray

[Tag pre]
# This style is used for multi-line verbatim blocks
foreground=#2e3436
foreground[darktheme] = lightgray

[Tag link]
foreground=blue
foreground[darktheme]=#99c3ff

[Tag page-link]
foreground=blue
foreground[darktheme]=#99c3ff

Plugins:
Insert Equation
勾选Use font color for dark theme
